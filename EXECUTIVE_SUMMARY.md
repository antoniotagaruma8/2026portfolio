## The Vibe & Overview
Forget the boring, stiff resumes. The 2026 Developer Portfolio is a neon-tinted, interactive playground designed to showcase my journey bridging education and tech. Built with a vibrant, playful glassmorphism aesthetic, this isn't just a static page—it's a digital experience packed with bouncing animations, hover reveals, and scroll-triggered interactions. It also features a built-in AI Chat Assistant infused with my exact persona, ready to vibe with potential employers and answer inquiries in real-time.

## The High-Performance Tech Stack
This project runs on cutting-edge tools tuned for maximum performance and buttery-smooth eye candy.

### Frontend & Interactive Engine
*   **Next.js 16 (App Router):** The powerhouse for server-side rendering and hyper-fast routing.
*   **React 19 & TypeScript 5:** For interactive, bug-free, and type-safe UI components.
*   **Tailwind CSS v4:** Styling the playful-teenie aesthetic—think dark mode splashed with electric blues, hot pinks, and lime greens.
*   **Framer Motion:** The secret sauce for all the movements! Expect fluid page transitions, springy bento-grid hovers, magnetic buttons, and infinite scroll animations.

### AI Brains
*   **Vercel AI SDK & Google Gemini (1.5 Flash) / Groq:** Powering the `/api/chat` route. My digital twin streams lightning-fast, smart responses to recruiters using a custom system prompt.

### Content & Deployment
*   **MDX (`next-mdx-remote`, `gray-matter`):** For turning rich-text markdown case studies into dynamic React components.
*   **next-themes & Lucide React:** For crisp icons and a flawless light/dark mode switch.
*   **Vercel:** Because we want deployment as fast as our animations.
