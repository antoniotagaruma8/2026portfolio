The <strong>Philippine Education System</strong> project is a custom-built, interactive, web-based presentation designed to deliver a comprehensive overview of the educational landscape, culture, and systemic structures in the Philippines. Moving away from traditional slide decks (like PowerPoint or Google Slides), this project leverages web technologies to create a dynamic, highly customizable, and visually engaging educational tool.

It provides an in-depth look into the country's K-12 revolution, bilingual language policies, deeply ingrained school culture, student life, and the unique challenges faced by the system, specifically tailored for an international audience.
