<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
<strong>Cold War Archives: Declassified</strong> is an interactive, browser-based educational game designed to teach the sociopolitical, economic, and geopolitical history of the Philippines during the Cold War era. Disguised as a retro-futuristic terminal interface, the application challenges students to "decrypt" classified intelligence files by unscrambling historical sentences. 

By gamifying the learning process, the project transforms dense historical data—from the Hukbalahap Rebellion and Martial Law to the Bell Trade Act and the EDSA Revolution—into an engaging, tactile puzzle experience.
</div>

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Core Features & Mechanics</h2>

The application transforms rote vocabulary sorting into an active learning experience:
<div className="space-y-8 my-8">
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Touch-Optimized Drag & Drop</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Built-in touch event handlers (`touchstart`, `touchmove`, `touchend`) alongside standard mouse events provide a universal, frustration-free interactive experience on all devices.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Dynamic Content Delivery</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    The game engine iterates through a curated JSON array of 40 historical events, dynamically generating word pools and validating user inputs. Built-in controls allow users to re-scramble words if they get stuck.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">

  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Terminal green accents, custom typography (`Courier New`), and reactive answer zones (turning green for correct, red for incorrect) provide immediate visual feedback loops for the learner.
  </div>
</div>
