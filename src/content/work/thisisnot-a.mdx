<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
<strong>This is not a...</strong> is a dynamic, browser-based interactive application designed for engaging group activities, educational gamification, and creative presentations. At its core, the application is a digital drawing board where users are prompted to sketch randomized objects. 

However, it elevates the standard drawing experience by introducing a high-pressure "Presentation Mode" requiring users to incorporate randomized vocabulary words into a timed speech, making it an excellent tool for language learning, icebreakers, or farewell events.

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Value Proposition</h2>

<strong>This is not a...</strong> acts as a powerful educational utility. The presentation mode forces users to construct sentences on the fly using targeted vocabulary, bridging the gap between creative expression and linguistic fluency. It also serves as an excellent low-stakes icebreaker where users can showcase their creativity and public speaking skills.
