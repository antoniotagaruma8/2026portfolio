The <strong>Philippine Education System</strong> presentation is an interactive, web-based educational tool developed as a final project for a Massive Open Online Course (MOOC). Foregoing traditional, static slide decks, this project utilizes a custom-built web interface to deliver a dynamic, engaging, and culturally rich overview of the educational landscape in the Philippines.

Designed with an international audience in mind (specifically providing comparative context to the Spanish educational system, such as aligning Senior High School with <em>Bachillerato</em>), the presentation navigates through the structural evolution of the country's system, its bilingual policies, embedded values, and the resilience of its students amidst socio-economic challenges.
