<strong>Lesson Hub</strong> is a dynamic, full-stack web application designed to modernize the way educators organize, manage, and share their daily teaching schedules and classroom resources. Moving beyond static paper planners and disjointed file systems, Lesson Hub provides a centralized digital dashboard.

The application is engineered to accommodate the dynamic, high-pressure nature of teaching:
