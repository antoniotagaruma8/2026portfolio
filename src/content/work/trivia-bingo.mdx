<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
<strong>Brain-Go Blitz!</strong> is a high-fidelity, real-time interactive web application designed to merge the classic, fast-paced mechanics of Bingo with rigorous educational trivia. Specifically targeted at secondary school and high school students, the platform serves as a dynamic learning tool where educators can host live game sessions to assess students' comprehension across various subjects in a highly gamified, competitive environment.

By combining the thrill of live multiplayer gaming with robust educational content, Brain-Go Blitz! ensures that formative assessment feels less like a test and more like an engaging classroom event.

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Gamification & Core Mechanics</h2>

The platform introduces strategic gameplay to deter guessing and reward knowledge:
<div className="space-y-8 my-8">
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">The Bingo Board & Interactions</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Students receive a randomized 5x5 Bingo card. To mark a drawn number, they must answer the broadcasted multiple-choice trivia question correctly.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">The Punishment System</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    If a student answers incorrectly, the cell's number is replaced. If no replacements are available, the cell is permanently "Burned" (❌), rewarding accuracy over speed.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Streaks & Power-Ups</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Consecutive correct answers activate point multipliers. Harder "Boss Questions" reward critical Power-Ups like Ghost (Skip), 50/50, and Hack (Auto-mark).
  </div>
</div>
</div>
