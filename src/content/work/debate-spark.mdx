<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Value Proposition & Use Cases</h2>

Whether deployed in a formal classroom setting, an ESL/EFL environment, or a youth group activity, Debate Spark provides immediate value by removing the friction of organizing a debate. The integrated keyboard shortcuts (`Space` to generate, `T` to toggle timer, `S` to spin sides) allow educators to act as seamless game-show hosts. By synthesizing high-end UI design with essential speech communication practice, the platform proves that academic rigor and gamified engagement can coexist beautifully.
