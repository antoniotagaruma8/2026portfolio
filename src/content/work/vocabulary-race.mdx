The <strong>Vocabulary Race Activity</strong> is a high-fidelity, interactive, browser-based educational game designed to gamify vocabulary acquisition and review. Engineered specifically for large displays (such as classroom TVs or interactive whiteboards), the platform transforms standard language learning into an engaging, competitive multiplayer experience. By challenging students to recall and input vocabulary words under time pressure, the system enhances engagement, retention, and collaborative learning dynamics.

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Strategic Trajectory</h2>

The Vocabulary Race Activity successfully bridges the gap between traditional vocabulary drills and modern gamified learning. The immediate functionality provides a powerful, zero-install classroom tool for educators, while the planned V2 architecture paves the way for a distributed, cloud-hosted SaaS platform where students can join via their own devices using unique lobby codes.
