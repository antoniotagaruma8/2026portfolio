<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
<strong>"Spanish Wit & Wisdom!"</strong> is a highly interactive, beautifully designed educational web application that helps users learn Spanish idioms, proverbs, and riddles. Unlike traditional flashcard applications, it employs a unique four-step progressive reveal mechanism that walks the user from the original Spanish phrase, to its literal translation, its English equivalent, and finally its underlying meaning. 

Built entirely with a lightweight vanilla frontend stack, the application features an ultra-vibrant, gamified "cartoony" aesthetic, smooth micro-interactions, and synthesized audio feedback, making language learning an engaging and memorable experience.

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Value Proposition</h2>

By providing both the *literal* translation and the *meaning*, the app teaches learners not just vocabulary, but the cultural logic behind Spanish expressions. The vibrant design, step-by-step reveals, and audio feedback transform rote memorization into an interactive mini-game, while seamless spacebar integration maximizes study efficiency.
