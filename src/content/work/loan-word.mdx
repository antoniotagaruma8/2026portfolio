<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
<strong>LoanWord Detectives</strong> is an interactive, gamified drag-and-drop educational tool designed to help learners explore the etymological origins of Filipino (Tagalog) vocabulary. By taking on the role of "detectives," students analyze linguistic "evidence cards" and categorize words into three historical origins: Indigenous (Austronesian), Spanish, and English. 

The application combines fast-paced sorting mechanics, real-time feedback, and competitive team modes to transform historical linguistics and vocabulary acquisition into an engaging classroom or solo activity.

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Gamification Mechanics</h2>

The application transforms rote vocabulary sorting into an active learning experience:
<div className="space-y-8 my-8">
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Dynamic Batch Dealing</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    To prevent screen crowding, the engine serves "evidence cards" in batches of 5. New cards are automatically dealt only when the current pile is cleared, managing cognitive load.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Instant Feedback Loop</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Correct placement generates a green floating "+100" text animation and locks the card in place. Incorrect placement triggers a red floating "-20" penalty and a visual "shake" animation, returning the card to the active state.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Multiplayer Modes & Time-Attack</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Supports "Solo Mission" alongside 2, 3, or 4-team competitive modes. Features a countdown timer that visually stresses (turns red) during the final 10 seconds to increase engagement.
  </div>
</div>
</div>
