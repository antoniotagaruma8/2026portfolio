<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
<strong>WordSnake</strong> is a fast-paced, competitive educational web application designed to test vocabulary recall, spelling, and quick thinking. Functioning as a high-stakes "category sprint," the game challenges two teams to build the longest continuous word chain before the timer runs out. Every new word must begin with the last letter of the previous word and strictly adhere to the chosen thematic category.

Built with a focus on versatile deployment in educational and team-building environments, WordSnake offers a dual-mode architecture: a <strong>Board Mode</strong> for facilitating traditional physical whiteboard play, and a fully digital <strong>Smart TV Mode</strong> featuring an interactive split-screen interface with built-in validation and on-screen keyboards.
</div>

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Project Vision & Utility</h2>

WordSnake bridges the gap between traditional classroom vocabulary games and modern digital interactives. By offering a clean, distraction-free **Board Mode**, it acts as an elegant timer and prompt generator for physical play. Conversely, the **Smart TV Mode** transforms the application into a standalone digital arena. 

With its high-fidelity UI, rigorous input validation, and competitive mechanics, WordSnake is an ideal tool for ESL/EFL classrooms, corporate icebreakers, and casual party environments.
