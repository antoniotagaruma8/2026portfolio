<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
<strong>Passive Voice Tug of War</strong> is an immersive, real-time multiplayer classroom game designed to help B2-level English language learners master the complexities of the Passive Voice. Operating as an interactive "Classroom Arcade" experience, the application transforms traditional grammar drills into a highly competitive, team-based digital tug of war. 

Powered by a robust Node.js and Socket.IO backend, the game splits the classroom into Red and Blue teams. Students use their mobile devices as game controllers, answering time-pressured grammar questions to "pull" a digital rope in their team's direction.
</div>

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Gamification & Interactive Mechanics</h2>

The platform features distinct gameplay phases designed to maximize student engagement and collaborative competition:
<div className="space-y-8 my-8">
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Tug of War Engine</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Live, synchronized questions are broadcast to all player devices. Correct answers from team members calculate a "pull strength" percentage, dynamically shifting a visual rope on the host screen toward the winning team.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Anti-Pattern Shuffling</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    The system automatically shuffles multiple-choice options on a per-question basis. This prevents pattern-guessing and ensures that students engage with the grammatical content.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">"Breaking News" Phase</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    A specialized visual mode contextualizing the passive voice in real-world journalism. Students view AI-generated news images with active facts and must select the correct passive headline transformation.
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<h2 className="section-label mt-12">Technical Architecture & UI/UX</h2>

The application is built using a modern, real-time web stack, optimized for instantaneous classroom interactivity and scalable deployment.
<div className="space-y-8 my-8">
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Real-Time Communication</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    A Node.js and Express backend combined with Socket.IO handles high-frequency, bidirectional state synchronization between the Host dashboard and dozens of Player clients, calculating aggregated scoring and rope physics in real-time.
  </div>
</div>
<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 md:gap-8 border-l-2 border-accent-blue/30 pl-4 md:pl-6 ml-2 md:ml-0 group">
  <div className="min-w-[220px] shrink-0 pt-1">
    <div className="text-sm font-bold text-foreground group-hover:text-accent-blue transition-colors">Deployment & Networking</div>
  </div>
  <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed">
    Built-in support for Fly.io Docker deployments and local Cloudflare tunneling (`cloudflared`), enabling teachers to launch a globally accessible game session directly from their localhost. Built-in QR code generation allows instant classroom onboarding.
  </div>

</div>
