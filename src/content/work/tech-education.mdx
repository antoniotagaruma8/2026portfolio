<div className="text-base text-muted-foreground leading-relaxed max-w-3xl mb-12">
The <strong>"Tech in Education"</strong> project is an innovative learning design for 4th-year ESO students that transforms a traditional web development unit into an immersive digital agency model. By utilizing generative AI as a developmental co-pilot, students bypass syntax barriers to focus on acting as project managers, UX designers, and system architects. 

This approach empowers students to build a professional, sustainable tourism web portal highlighting their community's maritime heritage, elevating the learning experience to the "Redefinition" level of the SAMR model.
</div>
