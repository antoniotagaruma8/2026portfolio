The <strong>ESO Smart Lesson Planner (ESOSLPv2)</strong> is a state-of-the-art educational technology platform engineered to revolutionize the lesson planning workflow for educators at the ESO (Educación Secundaria Obligatoria) and Bachillerato levels. By synthesizing advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) with rigorous pedagogical frameworks, ESOSLPv2 automates the generation of highly specialized, curriculum-aligned educational materials. 

Its core vision is to drastically reduce teacher workload—transforming hours of preparation into a streamlined process—while simultaneously elevating the quality, engagement, and personalization of classroom instruction.
