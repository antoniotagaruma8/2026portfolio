🎓 Research Data Collection & Consent

This application has been uniquely developed as part of a Master's Thesis research project: "AI for Differentiated Instruction: Evaluating a Content Generation Tool for Teacher Efficiency in Spanish Public Secondary Education."

Objective of the Study: The main objective of this study is to evaluate the effectiveness of AI-generated content in reducing the time teachers spend on lesson planning, while exploring its impact on enabling differentiated instruction in Spanish public secondary education.

  • Data Collected: By signing in and utilizing this tool, you consent to the collection of your non-sensitive usage data: Timestamp, Requested Topics, Year Level, CEFR Level, and Email Address.
  • Objective & Use: Data will only be used for the research purposes stipulated in this description. It will be used purely empirically to measure lesson preparation time reduction and evaluate the tool's impact on educational differentiation.
  • Anonymity & Confidentiality: Your participation data will be strictly confidential and anonymized in any published findings. Your personal generation logs and email address will not be shared publicly.
  • Storage & Duration: Your data will be securely stored on restricted-access cloud databases. It will be retained only for the duration of the Master's Thesis research and evaluation period, after which it will be securely and permanently deleted.
  • Data Controller: The principal researcher of this project is solely in charge of securely managing, handling, and deleting this data.
  • Right to Withdraw: Your participation is completely voluntary. You have the right to withdraw your consent and request the immediate deletion of your data at any time without any explanation or consequences.
